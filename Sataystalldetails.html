Description

This satay stall specializes in freshly grilled skewers of marinated meat, including chicken, beef, and mutton. Each skewer is cooked over charcoal to achieve a smoky aroma and tender texture. The satay is served with a rich, homemade peanut sauce, ketupat (rice cakes), sliced cucumbers, and onions.
